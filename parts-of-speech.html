Parts of Speech

Lugha huundwa kwa maelfu ya maneno ambayo huwekwa katika makundi yaitwayo Parts of Speech. Parts of speech ni makundi ya maneno kulingana na nafasi zake au jinsi yanavyotumika katika kuunda lugha.

Parts of speech ni makundi ya maneno kulingana na nafasi zake au jinsi yanavyotumika katika kuunda lugha. Baadhi ya maneno yanaweza kutumika katika makundi tofauti (different parts of speech) kulingana na matumizi. Maneno yote katika lugha yanaingia katika haya makundi na yana nafasi tofauti katika muundo wa lugha.

Examples:

Noun, Pronoun, Verb, Adjective, Adverb, Preposition, Conjunction, Interjection

Nouns

Introduction

Nouns (nomino) ni maneno ambayo ni majina ya watu, wanyama, mahali, wazo, hisia na vitu vingine

Examples

dog, Juma, love, car, Dar-es-Salaam, courage, bus, respect, faith, bicycle

Pronouns

Introduction

Pronouns hutumika kuwakilisha noun au kikundi cha maneno kinachotumika kama noun kuepusha kurudia rudia kuitaja noun.

Examples

Joshua is a good boy. He gets up early in the morning. (He ni pronoun ya Joshua inatumika kuepuka kurudia jina Joshua katika sentensi ya pili)

Exercise

Verbs

Introduction

Verb ni neno au maneno yanayoonesha kitendo, hali, au tukio. Verb katika sensensi hutumika kuonesha kinachofanywa na mtendaji (subject)

Examples

  • James walks in the morning. (walks inaonesha kitendo)
  • This is a good dog. (is inaonesha hali)
  • I sleep at night. (sleep inaonesha hali)
  • Samia became the president. (became inaonesha tukio)
  • I am happy. (am inaonesha hali)

Exercise

Adjectives

Introduction

Adjectives hutumika kutoa taarifa zaidi kuhusu nouns au pronouns. Hujibu maswali which one, what kind, or how many.

Examples:

  • There was an old man at the hospital. (old inaelezea zaidi kuhusu noun man)
  • The fruit looks ripe.
  • I met someone interesting.

Exercise

Adverbs

Introduction

Adverbs hutumika kutoa taarifa zaidi kuhusu adjectives, verbs, au adverbs nyingine. Kawaida adverbs huishia na -ly japokuwa sio zote. Hujibu maswali When? Where? How? or How much?

Examples:

  • He is running fast
  • She always reads attentively.
  • It is very tall.
  • He walked slowly.

Exercise

Prepositions

Introduction

Prepositions hutumika kuonesha uhusiano kati ya nouns na nouns au pronouns nyingine katika sentensi.

Examples:

  • I was in the classroom this morning. (in inaonesha wapi)
  • Go down the straight road.
  • He went across the road.
  • Take the car with you

Exercise

Conjunctions

Introduction

Conjunction hutumika kama kiunganishi kati ya nouns, vikundi vya maneno vinayotumika kama nouns (noun phrases), vikundi vya maneno vyenye kitenzi na kuwakilisha wazo fulani (clauses), au kiunganishi kati ya sentensi.

Examples:

  • And
  • As
  • Because
  • But
  • For
  • Just as
  • Or
  • Neither
  • Nor
  • Not only
  • So
  • Whether
  • Yet

Exercise

Interjections

Introduction

Interjection hutumika kunesha hisia au msisimuko katika sentensi.

Examples:

  • Good! Lets start the next one.
  • Oh! What a suprise.
  • Awsome! now let me try.
  • Indeed, this is how we do it.
  • Yes, I am looking forward to that.
